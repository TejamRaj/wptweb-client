Let’s break this paradox.. Work from Home ? or work from office?

Re-imagine future of “work culture “in the earth sized single workplace("WoT").

    Workplace on Tour(“WoT”) would be a next generation business (or commercial) work platform which offers remote workplace in tourist destination areas across the globe.

It will allow people (formally employees) to book a workplace for 3-4 weeks of time [may be more] in tour destinations areas, enable them to explore their favourite places in between their weekly off schedule, do exciting activities like boat sailing, river rafting, sky diving etc. [I named few].

Enabling such way of ‘work style’ in modern [gig economy] world would be a game changing move to get over office/home anxiety, work pressure. and tackle people well being challenge. It will also open countless opportunities in straightening world business economy.

And yes, all this possible with the power of cloud computing which redefined morden Business landscape.
